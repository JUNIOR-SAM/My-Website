ABOUT US

Hi, I'm Samuel Oyebode, a passionate cybersecurity student at SQI College of ICT with a strong creative side as a graphic designer. I love blending the precision of tech with the freedom of design—whether I’m securing networks or crafting bold, user-friendly visuals.

My journey into tech began with curiosity and a laptop. Over time, that curiosity grew into real skills: writing clean code, designing professional flyers, logos, and interfaces, and learning how to protect systems from digital threats. Now, I’m not just learning cybersecurity—I’m building my brand by combining it with creative problem-solving.

As a designer, I use tools like Adobe Photoshop, Pixellab, and Canva to bring ideas to life. On the tech side, I’m diving deep into ethical hacking, network security, and scripting basics. I believe in constant learning, creativity with purpose, and using technology to make meaningful impact. Whether you’re looking for a secure solution or a standout design, I’m here to bring clarity, creativity, and commitment to the work.
